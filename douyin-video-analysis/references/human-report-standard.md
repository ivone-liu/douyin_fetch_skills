# Human report standard

A good human-facing report is not a JSON dump.

It should include:

1. One-sentence verdict
2. Story hypothesis
3. Emotional conflict
4. Hook mechanism
5. Editing and pacing
6. Shot/timeline breakdown
7. Dialogue/script reconstruction with confidence labels
8. Why the metrics are high
9. What is reusable
10. What still needs ASR or multimodal verification

Minimum quality bar:
- useful to a human creator
- clear about evidence vs inference
- detailed enough to support recreation
