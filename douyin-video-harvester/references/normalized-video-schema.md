# Normalized video schema

Required fields:
- `video_id`
- `author_sec_user_id`
- `author_unique_id`
- `author_name`
- `desc`
- `create_time`
- `crawl_time`

Recommended engagement fields:
- `digg_count`
- `comment_count`
- `collect_count`
- `share_count`
- `play_count`

Recommended media fields:
- `cover_url`
- `play_url`
- `duration_ms`
- `music_title`
- `music_author`

Operational fields:
- `raw_page_cursor`
- `source_endpoint`
- `incomplete_reason`
